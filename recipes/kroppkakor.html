Kroppkakor - Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 kg potatoes
  • water to boil potatoes in
  • salt to the water
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tsk salt

Stuffing

  • 150 g pork
  • 1 onion of choice
  • 1 krm whitepeppar
  • 1/2 tsk spicy peppar, crushed

Boiling

  • 2 liter water
  • 1 msk salt

Steps

  1. Peel and rinse the potatoes. Boil it in salted water until soft. Drain the water and steam the potatoes.
  2. Press it through a puree press or mash it with a potato masher or electric beater. Let the potatoes cool.
  3. Cut the pork for the filling into small cubes. Peel and finely chop the onion. Brown the pork in a pan. Add the onion and let everything sizzle for a while.
  4. Season with pepper and let the filling cool.
  5. Mix the mashed potatoes with lightly beaten egg, flour and salt. Don't touch too much.
  6. Form the dough into a roll and cut it into about 12 pieces (for 4 ports). Make an indentation in each piece and add a scant tablespoon of the filling.
  7. Press the dough together over the filling and form into round, slightly flat buns. Boil water and salt.
  8. Add a few body cakes at a time and cook them for 10-12 minutes, or about 5 minutes after they have risen.
